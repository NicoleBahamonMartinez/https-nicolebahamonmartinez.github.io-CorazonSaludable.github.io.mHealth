m-Health for CVD

Mobile Health applied to the prevention and detection of cardiovascular diseases.

Article distribution per type

112 full text articles were retrieved. After applying the exclussion criteria 71 articles remained. Most of them were either Systematic Literature Reviews, Surveys or Trials.

For all of the articles the following data was extracted

  • Type of study
  • Type of nudge evaluated
  • Targeted risk factor
  • CVD focused
  • mHealth usage
  • Primary or Secondary Prevention
  • Demographic variable
  • Target user: Patients, Caregivers or Providers
  • Additional comments

Systematic Literature Reviews and Surveys

For articles of this category the following data was extracted
  • Number of articles evaluated
  • Number of articles per risk factor
  • Number of articles per type of nudge
  • Number of articles with significant results
  • Number of articles with long term evaluation of nudges
  • Time frame evaluated

Controlled Trials, Randomized Controlled Trials and Non-Randomized Controlled Trials

CT , RCT and NRCT

For articles of this category the following data was extracted

  • Number of participants per intervention
  • Number of participants per control group
  • Performance metrics
  • Intervention evaluated
